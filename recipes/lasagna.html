Lasagna

Freshly baked lasagna.

Great recipe to cook for a family of 4. The recipe utilises fresh ingredients and can easily be cooked in under 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Passata
  • Milk
  • Bay Leaves

Step by Step

  1. Cook Pasta Sheets
  2. Brown beef in pan
  3. Add Passata
  4. Combine everything.
